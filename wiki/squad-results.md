## Summary
BERT achieves state-of-the-art results on SQuAD 1.1 and 2.0, outperforming previous models like ELMo and GPT.

## Explanation
On SQuAD 1.1, BERTBASE achieves 88.5 F1 and BERTLARGE reaches 91.8 F1, surpassing prior systems. For SQuAD 2.0, which allows for no-answer cases, BERTLARGE (Ensemble) scores 93.2 F1. These results demonstrate BERT's effectiveness in understanding and generating contextually relevant answers. [[model-size-impact]]
