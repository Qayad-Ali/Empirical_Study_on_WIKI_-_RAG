## Summary
BERT is pre-trained on two tasks: Masked Language Model (MLM) and Next Sentence Prediction (NSP), which help it learn contextual representations.

## Explanation
The Masked Language Model task involves predicting randomly masked words in a sentence, forcing the model to understand context from both directions. The Next Sentence Prediction task learns to determine if two sentences are consecutive in the original text, improving understanding of document-level context. These tasks are crucial for developing the model's ability to handle complex linguistic patterns. [[bidirectional-training]]
