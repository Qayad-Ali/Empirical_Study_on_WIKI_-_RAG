## Summary
Ablation studies show that both pre-training tasks (MLM and NSP) are essential for BERT's performance.

## Explanation
Removing the NSP task significantly harms performance on tasks like MNLI and SQuAD, indicating its importance for understanding document-level context. Similarly, unidirectional models (like OpenAI GPT) perform worse than bidirectional models, highlighting the necessity of bidirectional training. [[pre-training-tasks]]
