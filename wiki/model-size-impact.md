## Summary
Larger BERT models (BERTLARGE) consistently outperform smaller variants (BERTBASE) across multiple tasks.

## Explanation
Increasing the number of layers, hidden units, and attention heads leads to significant accuracy improvements on tasks like MNLI and GLUE. For example, BERTLARGE achieves 93.3% accuracy on SST-2, compared to 92.9% for BERTBASE. This demonstrates that scaling model size enhances performance, even on small-scale tasks.
